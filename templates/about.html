About WaterLemon

We are a group of Artificial Intelligence students at the UAB who decided to create a project based on putting watermarks to AI. The objective is that people can easily detect when a LLM has intervened in the creation of some text.

Even though we cannot put a watermark on every Large Language Model, we have created this webpage to show our idea and try to promote its use. We have used a simple and open-source model.

For more details and contributions, visit our GitHub repository.
